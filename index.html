Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Market Tracker ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the App collects, uses and safeguards your data.

Data stored on your device

Your watchlist, portfolio positions, trade history, alerts and preferences are stored locally on your device using browser storage (localStorage). This data remains on your device and is not transmitted unless you enable Cloud Backup (see below).

Cloud backup (optional)

When you enable the Cloud Backup feature in Settings, your portfolio data (watchlists, positions, alerts, closed trade history and preferences) is transmitted and stored on our secure cloud servers. This data is associated with a randomly generated device identifier — not your name, email or any personal information. Cloud Backup is disabled by default and only activates when you explicitly enable it. You may disable it at any time and no further data will be transmitted. You can delete your backup data anytime by clicking on "Delete cloud backup".

Push notifications and device registration

When you enable notifications, the App registers your device with a push notification service to deliver price alerts. A device token and a randomly generated device identifier are stored on our servers to route notifications. No personal information (name, email, phone number) is collected. You may disable notifications at any time in Settings, which stops all push notifications and marks your device as inactive on our servers.

Price alerts

When you create price alerts, the alert conditions (symbol, target price, threshold) are synced to our servers so they can be checked periodically even when the App is closed. Alert data is associated with your device identifier only.

Third-party data sources

The App retrieves market data from third-party financial data providers and public government databases. This includes stock and cryptocurrency prices, financial filings, insider transactions, currency exchange rates, treasury yields and market sentiment indices.

Personal information

The App does not collect, store or process any personally identifiable information. There are no user accounts, no email collection, no analytics tracking, no advertising SDKs and no third-party tracking scripts. The only identifier used is a randomly generated device ID stored locally on your device.

Data sharing

The App does not sell, share, rent or transmit your data to any third party for marketing, analytics or any other purpose. Data sent to our servers (alerts, device tokens, backups) is used solely to provide the App's functionality. The export feature (Excel) creates a file on your device which you control entirely.

Data retention and deletion

Local data is retained on your device until you clear the App's data or uninstall it. Server-side data (alerts, device registration, backups) is retained as long as needed to provide the service. You may request deletion of all server-side data associated with your device by contacting us at the address below.

Proprietary algorithms

The App processes publicly available financial news headlines through proprietary algorithms to generate sentiment scores. No user-specific data is used in this process. The algorithmic methodology, scoring logic and underlying source code are confidential intellectual property of JorgeGF and may not be reverse-engineered, copied or reproduced.

Children's privacy

The App is not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the App with an updated date. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
